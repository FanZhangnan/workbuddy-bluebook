# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation respectful and accessible regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Be constructive, respect privacy and consent, distinguish evidence from opinion, accept editorial review, and focus critique on the work. Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, publication of private information, and bad-faith manipulation are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and participation that violate these standards. Reports should be sent privately to the maintainers through repository moderation channels. Maintainers will protect reporter privacy where possible and apply proportionate corrective action.

This compact policy is adapted from Contributor Covenant 2.1.
